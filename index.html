Who We Are

Earth Explored brings planetary data to your fingertips—melding weather, history, flight paths, and user-generated content in one seamless interactive globe. Our platform empowers students, researchers, travelers, and curious minds to uncover insights about our planet in real time, with beautiful visuals and intuitive controls.

Key Features

Interactive Weather Maps

Visualize real-time and historical weather with high-res overlays and time-slider controls.

Historical Timelines

Journey through volcanic eruptions, ancient empires, and world events—mapped on a 3D globe.

Global Flight Tracker

Track thousands of flights in real time: routes, altitudes, aircraft details, and delay stats.
