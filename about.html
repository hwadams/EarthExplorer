Our Story

Founded in 2023, Earth Explored began as a student project at Global University. Today, we partner with NASA, NOAA, national archives, and thousands of contributors worldwide to layer real-time weather, historical events, flight & ship movements, and geotagged imagery onto a single, interactive globe.

Why We Exist

In an era of data silos and fractured tools, our goal is to bring everything into one place. Teachers use us in classrooms, researchers in labs, and travelers on the go—all via web or mobile app.

  • Instant global context
  • High-resolution, up-to-date layers
  • Community-driven photo galleries
